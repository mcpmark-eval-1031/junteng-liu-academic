Junteng Liu is a first-year PhD candidate at HKUST NLP Group, researching natural language processing and machine learning. His interests include LLM reasoning, reinforcement learning, hallucination in vision-language models, and LLM truthfulness. He holds a B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and has interned at MINIMAX, Tencent WXG, and Shanghai AI Lab. He has published papers at EMNLP, ICML, and NeurIPS.
